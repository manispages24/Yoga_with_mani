Welcome to Yoga with Imani! As a certified yoga instructor, I specialize in teaching various forms of yoga
that promote relaxation and meditation. Whether you're looking to unwind after a long day or simply
want to take some time for yourself, my classes in Yin Yoga, Gentle Yoga, and Chair Yoga are designed to
help you achieve a state of calm and clarity. With a focus on slow, mindful movements and deep breathing techniques,
my classes will allow you to release tension and find balance both physically and mentally.
Join me on the mat and discover the transformative power of yoga for yourself.
